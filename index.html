Guelph, a vibrant community in southern Ontario with over 120,000 residents, holds a rich history dating back to its founding in 1827. Interestingly, the land on which Guelph stands was utilized by Native Americans over 11,000 years ago. Today, the city is celebrated for its picturesque and diverse natural beauty, featuring well-maintained trails that allow residents and visitors to explore its lush surroundings. Guelph takes pride in its commitment to environmental sustainability, demonstrated through ongoing efforts to preserve green spaces, enhancing the city's overall ecological richness.

I'm part of the IT Service Desk team at City Hall, where I collaborate with a diverse group of individuals responsible for maintaining various aspects of the city. From building planners to the police and fire department, as well as other infrastructure services, our team plays a crucial role in supporting the people who ensure the smooth operation of the city.
